History of the basketball

section Basketball is FC Barcelona’s most important professional section, both socially and in a sporting sense, and is the second favorite team of all the Club’s fans Founded on 24 August 1926, the FC Barcelona basketball team played their first games at the Sol de Baix sports complex (between the current Avinguda Madrid and the Travessera de Les Corts).
It was not until the 1930s that basketball became well established in Catalonia. After the Spanish Civil War, the FC Barcelona basketball team played in the First Division (1940) on a new court next to the south end of the Les Corts football field. The team’s best era would come in the second half of the 1940s, with Barça firmly seated high up the ladder of Spanish basketball..
